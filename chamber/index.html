Quito Chamber of Commerce

Connecting Businesses. Growing Community.

The Quito Chamber of Commerce is the trusted voice of local businesses. We connect entrepreneurs, corporations, and community members to promote economic growth, innovation, and prosperity.

Target Market

Business owners and patrons in the city of Quito and surrounding areas.

Site Goals

  • Foster deeper connections with members by offering valuable resources, professional development opportunities, and interactive networking events that help businesses grow and collaborate.
  • Support the expansion of existing businesses, attract new investments, and encourage entrepreneurship to build a stronger, more resilient local economy.
  • Position the Chamber as the leading voice for the business community, advocating on behalf of members and serving as a trusted partner for economic development initiatives.
  • Strengthen the bond between local businesses and residents by promoting local shopping, community events, and volunteer opportunities that enrich the quality of life in Quito.
  • Leverage digital tools, data, and modern communication strategies to keep members informed, attract younger entrepreneurs, and ensure the Chamber remains relevant in a fast-changing business environment.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1 - Local Business Owner:
    A small business owner is considering joining the Chamber of Commerce to connect with other entrepreneurs. They visit the website to learn about membership benefits, pricing options, and the simple steps required to apply.
  • Scenario 2 - Community Member:
    A resident is interested in participating in local activities and professional workshops organized by the Chamber. They use the website to explore the events calendar, read event details, and register online with ease.
  • Scenario 3 - Prospective Resident / Investor:
    A visitor from outside the city is evaluating Timbuktu as a potential place to live and invest. They navigate the Chamber’s website to research the local business environment, review available services, explore workforce qualifications, and get an overview of the community’s quality of life.
